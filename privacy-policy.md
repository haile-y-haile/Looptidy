# Privacy Policy for LoopTidy

**Effective Date:** June 3, 2026

## 1. Introduction
LoopTidy ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the LoopTidy application (the "App").

## 2. Local-First Architecture
LoopTidy is designed with a strict local-first architecture. This means:
- **No Cloud Syncing:** All data you enter into the App (including loops, decisions, people, and attachments) is stored entirely locally on your device using SQLite and the device's internal storage mechanisms.
- **No External Servers:** We do not operate backend servers that receive, process, or store your personal data or the content you create within the App.
- **No Third-Party Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (such as Google Analytics or Mixpanel) to track your behavior or usage of the App.

## 3. Data We Collect
Because of our local-first architecture, **we do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data** from you when you use the App.

The only exceptions are standard App Store crash logs and anonymous usage metrics provided natively by Apple (if you have opted-in to share these with developers through your iOS system settings). These are completely anonymous and cannot be linked to you or your data.

## 4. Permissions We Request
To provide certain features, the App may request access to native device capabilities:
- **Face ID / Touch ID (Biometrics):** LoopTidy requests permission to use local biometric authentication to secure access to the App. Your biometric data never leaves your device's Secure Enclave, and the App only receives a "success" or "fail" signal from the operating system. We do not have access to your face or fingerprint data.
- **Push Notifications:** The App requests permission to schedule local, on-device notifications to remind you of due dates or snoozed loops. These notifications are scheduled locally by your device; we do not use an external push notification server to send them.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
