# Privacy Policy — LoopTidy

**Last updated:** June 2026  
**Applies to:** LoopTidy for iOS (App Store)

**Contact:** hello.hailelabs@gmail.com  
**Public URL:** https://haile-y-haile.github.io/Looptidy/privacy

## Overview

LoopTidy is **local-first**. Loop data you create is stored on **your device**, not on LoopTidy-operated servers.

LoopTidy does **not**:

- Collect, sell, or share your personal data for advertising or profiling
- Operate backend servers that store your loop content
- Use in-app analytics SDKs
- Show ads
- Send remote push notifications from our servers
- Require a cloud account in this version

## What is stored on your device

- Open loop titles, descriptions, types, status, priority, and risk
- Due dates, reminders, notes, timeline events, and decisions
- Names or labels you enter for people tied to a loop
- Link URLs you attach to a loop
- Scope changes, feedback items, and weekly review data
- Appearance preferences, onboarding state, and optional app-lock setting

Deleting the app or using “Delete all local data” in Backup & Restore removes this data from your device.

## Permissions

- **Face ID / Touch ID (optional):** Used only if you enable App lock in Settings. Biometric data never leaves your device’s Secure Enclave.
- **Notifications (optional):** Used only when you set a local reminder on a loop. Notifications are scheduled on-device; we do not operate a push notification server.

## Third parties

### Apple (App Store)

Apple processes app installation and updates and may provide anonymous crash diagnostics under [Apple’s privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### Expo Application Services (maintainers only)

Production iOS builds may be produced with [EAS Build](https://expo.dev/eas). That process builds the app from source; it does not upload your on-device loop data.

## Children

LoopTidy is not directed at children under 13.

## Changes

If we add cloud sync, accounts, analytics, or remote services in a future version, this policy will be updated before those features ship in a meaningful way.
